Key Facts

Star topology: All devices connect to a central switch/hub. Easy to add devices. Central point of failure.
Mesh topology: Every device connects to every other. Highly redundant — no single point of failure. Expensive and complex.
Router connects different networks. Switch directs data within a network. WAP provides wireless access.
Exam tip: You need to describe advantages and disadvantages of star and mesh topologies, and explain the role of routers, switches and WAPs.
