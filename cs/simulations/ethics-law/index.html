Key Facts

Data Protection Act 2018 (UK GDPR) — governs how personal data is collected, stored, and used. Requires consent, security, and data minimisation.
Computer Misuse Act 1990 — makes it illegal to gain unauthorised access to computer systems, modify data without permission, or create/distribute malware.
Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 — protects creative and intellectual works including software, music, images, and text from unauthorised copying.
